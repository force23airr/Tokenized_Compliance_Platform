# Dashboard Modules

Specialized dashboards for different stakeholders.

## Regulator Portal

Read-only access for regulators (SEC, FCA, MAS) to monitor compliance in real-time.

**Features:**
- Cap table visibility
- Transfer history audit trail
- Automated regulatory filings
- Suspicious activity alerts
- Investor distribution by jurisdiction

**Why:** Makes compliance officer's life easy, builds trust with regulators.

## Audit Exports

One-click export packages for year-end audits and tax reporting.

**Supported Formats:**
- Big 4 audit firms (KPMG, PwC, Deloitte, EY)
- Tax reporting (K-1s, 1099s)
- Fund administrator reconciliation
- SEC/FINRA reporting

**Why:** Year-end close in hours, not weeks.
