# Legal Templates Module

Pre-built, jurisdiction-specific legal documents for RWA tokenization.

## Why This Matters

Competitors offer tech. We offer tech + legal docs = complete solution.
Clients go live in weeks, not months.

## Template Categories

### Private Placement Memorandums (PPM)
- US Reg D 506(b) PPM
- US Reg D 506(c) PPM
- UK FCA-compliant PPM
- Singapore SFA Section 275 PPM

### Subscription Agreements
- Individual investor (accredited)
- Entity investor (corporation/LLC)
- Trust investor
- Fund/LP investor

### Side Letters
- MFN provisions
- Co-investment rights
- Reporting requirements
- Fee arrangements

### Board Resolutions
- Token issuance authorization
- Compliance officer appointment
- Custodian selection
- Transfer agent designation
