# Private Placement Memorandum Templates

## Available Templates

- `us_reg_d_506b.md` - US Regulation D 506(b) offering
- `us_reg_d_506c.md` - US Regulation D 506(c) offering
- `uk_fca_compliant.md` - UK FCA compliant offering
- `sg_section_275.md` - Singapore SFA Section 275 offering

## Template Variables

All templates use JSON-based variable substitution. The AI compliance engine
automatically populates these based on asset and offering configuration.
